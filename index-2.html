TLT Key Features

By utilizing a unique temporal sequencing algorithm, TLT enables Dragon Network to process upwards of 1.5 million transactions per second, eclipsing traditional blockchain models.

TLT operates on a hybrid consensus mechanism that combines elements of Proof of Stake (PoS) and our novel Temporal Proof of Transaction (TPoT), drastically reducing the energy footprint compared to traditional Proof of Work systems.

TLT allows for more complex and efficient smart contracts. These contracts can execute operations based on time-based triggers, enabling a new realm of automated services.

Integrated with Draco AI, TLT benefits from real-time analytics and predictive modeling, offering unparalleled insights and decision-making assistance to network users.

Designed to accommodate rapid growth, the TLT framework supports scalable infrastructure developments, ensuring consistent performance despite increasing network demands.

With an emphasis on security, TLT incorporates advanced cryptographic techniques and continuous security audits, safeguarding the network's integrity and user assets.

Inspired by Solana's open-source ethos, Dragon Network's TLT will be developed and refined in collaboration with a global community of developers and users.
